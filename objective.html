Project Objective

Kathmandu is currenly regarded as one of the most polluted city globally. Air pollution, noise pollution, soil pollution etc. are just a few things that down this city's status; disregarding the actual beauty of it all. So this survey is conducted for the purpose of raising awareness to the local authority about the impact of pollution on the common Nepali living in the city and partially for helping foreign and/or domestic tourists get a better idea about the points of interests around Kathmandu; as I plan to vizualize the data I collect in some interactive way on the near future. I obviously need a lot of data for that to happen, so I request you to share this site with buddies who are living/have lived in Kathmandu. I think that this will work in the betterment of the community as a whole.
